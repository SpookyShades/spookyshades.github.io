## 1.0.0

*2025.12.20* for ATS/ETS2 **v1.57**

- Entirely refactored the layout of the website to make more easily accessible and visually appealing
- Added interactive ATS and ETS2 maps for streamlined navigation of the homepage
- Added a questions/answers page
- Removed the mileage target pages in favor of splitting targets by state directly
    - Due to this, pages have been created for Alberta, Arizona, California, Colorado, Idaho, Kentucky, Mexico, Minnesota, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, North Dakota, Oregon, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, Washington, and Wyoming for ATS, and Base Map ETS2, Going East!, Scandinavia, Vive la France!, Italia, Beyond the Baltic Sea, Road to the Black Sea, and Iberia for ETS2
- Created map pages for British Columbia and South Dakota, as they previously only had one version of the map that didn't require separate history pages
- Created map pages for Mississippi and Wisconsin, though both states presently lack any mileage targets
- Added bottom update bar for version-updated pages
- Added information button in the bottom corner
- Added crossing hyperlinks between states
- Added this changelog
